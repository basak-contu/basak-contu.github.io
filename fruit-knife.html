Fruit Slice
  Fruit Slice is a 3D, hyper-casual game developed in Unity. You must slice as many fruit as you can by throwing knives at them. You have limited knives so you must pick more knives by breaking boxes and picking knives inside them. But be careful because some boxes have a bomb inside them. If you blow up it up, you will lose some of your knives.
